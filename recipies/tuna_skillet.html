Tuna Skillet Casserole

A delicious tuna skillet that's easy to make!

Made with just some tuna, cream of mushroom, and frozen veggies, this dish is easy to make and very filling!

Ingredients

  • Diced Onion
  • Chicken Broth
  • Egg Noodles
  • Cream of Mushroom Soup
  • Frozen Peas and Carrots
  • Canned Tuna
  • Marbled Cheddar Cheese
  • Crushed Croutons

Steps

  1. Start by saut√©ing some onion in the pan until it`s nice and tender
  2. Add in the chicken broth and cream of mushroom soup and whisk until combined
  3. Stir in the egg noodles and frozen peas and carrots, then bring the mixture to a boil. Cook until the noodles are tender
  4. Flake the tuna and gently fold into the pasta. Cover with the shredded cheese then let simmer with the lid on until the cheese is melted and tuna is heated through
  5. Finally, crush up the croutons and sprinkle over the top
